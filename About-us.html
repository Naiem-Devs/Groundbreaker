Our History

Born from decades of real-world experience in the water industry, Groundbreaker Systems is all about solving the challenges that others overlook, delivering smart, sustainable solutions that make a tangible difference.

We don’t just create products; we create better ways of doing things, helping businesses and communities work more efficiently and responsibly with water.

Groundbreaker Systems – like our namesake innovation – is a business built to quite literally break new ground.

Our story begins with Steve Leigh, who spent over 30 years working in the water industry,  immersed in the practical, day-to-day realities of how utilities were being delivered. And he saw a problem: buried boundary boxes, access issues, high maintenance costs, inefficiencies across the board.

There had to be a better way. A smarter, more sustainable solution that made sense – not just on paper but on site. And so, Steve created one.

Innovation at Our Core  The original Groundbreaker Box changed everything. By taking water connection points out of the pavement and onto the wall of the property, it removed the need for street furniture, slashed installation time, improved meter access and offered a secure, future-ready platform for smart metering. It was a simple shift in thinking – but one that solved multiple long-standing problems in a single stroke.

And that spirit of practical innovation didn’t stop there.

In 2010, we introduced INSUduct and SHalloduct – both designed to help developers meet insulation requirements and streamline the replacement of lead supply pipes. Like the Groundbreaker box, these products were developed by listening to the people actually on site, understanding the problem and using our vast experience to make things better.

Next came the NRV2 and LoFlo, offering in-line flow restriction to help manage consumption and improve network efficiency. These tools support water-saving efforts at scale, allowing providers to respond to growing environmental pressures and industry-wide consumption targets.

What makes us different? We’re a small team with big ambitions. That means we can move fast – LoFlo went from idea to prototype in under a month – and we stay close to the ground, solving real-world problems with smart, elegant solutions that just work.
