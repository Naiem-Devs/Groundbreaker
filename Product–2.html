Groundbreaker

How can a small brown box deliver so much?

Meet our namesake: Groundbreaker. The UK’s only pre-insulated, surface-mounted water service connection box.

It’s a real game-changer in water management—designing out leakage, reducing street furniture and offering a secure platform for smart meters. Efficient, cost-effective and provides full-site protection against backflow.

The original – and still the benchmark. Groundbreaker is the UK’s first above-ground, wall-mounted water service solution. Designed to eliminate the need for buried boundary boxes, it simplifies installation, improves access, and provides whole-site backflow protection. Smart meter-ready, its modern infrastructure made easy.

Features & benefits

Fully accessible

Wall mounting allows for full access to water control and meters with ease reducing the need for bulky street furniture and highway liability.

As green as it is brown

Groundbreaker is made from reprocessed materials helping you meet your environmental responsibilities.

Hardwearing & durable

Our case is certified to resist temperatures of -15C for three days.

Backflow protection

Groundbreaker is certified to provide whole site protection against backflow to fluid category 2.

Totally versatile

It can be used on everything from newbuild and urban regeneration projects to mobile and modular homes.

Futureproof design

Our solution is compliant with and indeed exceeds all water legislation, it’s designed for life.
